Who We Are

A cooperative rooted in Israeli agriculture with a global trading perspective.

Beresheet is an agricultural cooperative founded by 17 kibbutzim and moshavim in the Galilee and Golan Heights. We combine strong farming roots, advanced packing and cooling infrastructure, and deep Israeli market knowledge.

What We Do

Managing the full fruit value chain from orchard to market.

We produce, pack, market and import apples, pears, cherries, kiwi, stone fruit, berries, pomegranates, persimmons and avocado. Our operations cover orchards, grower relations, quality control, cooling, logistics, marketing and distribution.
